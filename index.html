How To Make Mashed Potato


Mashed potatoes, is a dish made by mashing boiled potatoes, usually with added milk, butter, salt and pepper. It is generally served as a side dish to meat or vegetables. When the potatoes are only roughly mashed, they are sometimes called smashed potatoes. Dehydrated instant mashed potatoes and frozen mashed potatoes are available. Mashed potatoes are an ingredient in other dishes.

How To Make?

Ingredients

  1. Potato
  2. 120ml milk
  3. 2 tablespoon of butter
  4. 1/2 teaspoon off salt
  5. 1/2 teaspoon of black pepper
  6. Chopped celery leaves

step

  1. First wash and peel the potatoes, and then, slice them
  2. Boil the potatoes for about 15 minutes or 20 minutes
  3. After that we can drain well the potatoes, and then we mash the potatoes with a spoon or something else that can mash the potatoes.
  4. Now we move on the next step, heat the milk and butter until mixed
  5. And then pour the hot milk into the mashed potatoes, mix well
  6. And lastly, sprinkle with the celery and the black pepper
  7. And ready to serve
